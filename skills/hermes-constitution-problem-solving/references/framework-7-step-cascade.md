# SUB-FRAMEWORK: The 7-Step Cascade (The Path of Manifestation)
*A structural sub-framework mapping the flow from idea to completion.*

This cascade is used to secularize the flow of manifestation itself.

1. **Concept:** The seed, the raw input or idea.
2. **Philosophy:** The fundamental belief system or lens applied to the concept.
3. **Methodology:** The rationale or strategic operational approach derived from the philosophy (e.g., IPO).
4. **Framework:** The structural skeleton or map built to hold the operational approach (e.g., The Parent Trinity).
5. **Tactics:** The physical maneuvers, single muscular contractions, or specific deployment of tools within the framework.
6. **Procedures:** The rhythms and homeostasis; a sequence of tactics designed to navigate a problem or manage errors (Inflammation).
7. **Reality:** The final manifestation, output state, or digested conceptual material.
