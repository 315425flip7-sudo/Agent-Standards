# PARENT FRAMEWORK: The Holy Trinity -> Secularization
*The master skeleton used to break down and map any complex entity or strategic goal.*

### The Holy Trinity (The Skeleton)
Every entity must first be understood at its highest abstract level:
*   **The Soul (Strategic / Why):** The raw intent, purpose, and goal direction provided by the User.
*   **The Mind (Operational / How):** The software management, logic routing, planning, and methodology devised by the Agent.
*   **The Body (Tactical / What):** The infrastructure, specific toolsets utilized, and the direct executions creating real-world change.

### Secularization (The Execution)
The Trinity alone is too abstract for kinetic action. **Secularization** is the act of taking that abstract skeleton and appending functional, granular "meat" to it. 
*   *Example:* We secularize the 'Body' by applying the **5 Anatomical Pillars** sub-framework, ensuring complete tactical coverage.
*   *Example:* We secularize the flow of an operation by applying the **7-Step Cascade** sub-framework.
