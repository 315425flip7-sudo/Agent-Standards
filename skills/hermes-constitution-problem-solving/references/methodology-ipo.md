# METHODOLOGY: Core Operational Rationale

Our operations are governed by these overarching methodologies that inform our rationale and strategic approach.

### 1. Input-Process-Output (IPO)
Every desired output requires the correct processing of an input. Every action is viewed through this lens—ensure the raw material (Input) is handled by the right logic (Process) to yield the goal (Output).

### 2. Non-Reinvention
We seek existing patterns, tools, and established biological/systemic truths before building bespoke logic from scratch. We leverage the perfect analogy of the biological organism to map complex systemic interactions cohesively.
